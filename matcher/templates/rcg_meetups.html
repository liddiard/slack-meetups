University Programs presents RCG Meetups, a program to help you get to know other recent college grads through casual 1:1 chats. Meet new friends, grow your network, and form meaningful connections with RCGs from across the company.

Pairings happen every other week – you can opt in or out based on your availability. Just join #rcg-meetups to participate.

Join #rcg-meetups

How does the program work?

After joining the Slack channel, you’ll receive a message at the start of the next pairing round asking for an introduction.

After introducing yourself, you’ll receive your first pairing.

Message the RCG you pair with to meet up!

For following rounds, you’ll be asked if you want to be paired with someone new this week.

Pairing rounds start the first and third Monday of the month. If at any point you want to leave the program, simply leave the Slack channel.

Why RCG Meetups?

The goal of RCG Meetups is to to help you make new friends and grow your network at PayPal.

It can be difficult to get to know other recent college grads outside of quarterly events. Participating in RCG Meetups gives you more familiar faces the next time you’re at an RCG gathering.

1:1 meetups also help you gain insight into different parts of the company. You might even find someone you have a lot in common with who you wouldn’t have met otherwise!
